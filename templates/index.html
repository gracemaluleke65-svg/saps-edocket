About the System

The Future of Criminal Justice Workflow

SAPS eDocket is a secure, web-based digital case management platform built to replace vulnerable paper dockets. It empowers law enforcement officers with real‑time tracking, biometric verification, role‑based access, and comprehensive audit trails. Designed for the South African Police Service, it ensures integrity, transparency, and efficiency from case opening to conviction.

Our Core Services

Complete Case Management Ecosystem

Case Tracking

Facial Recognition
